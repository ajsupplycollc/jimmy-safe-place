Our Story

Two Kinds of Rescue.
One Safe Place.

Recovery is more than sobriety. It is about rediscovering purpose, building trust, and learning to care for something beyond yourself.

Jimmy Safe Place of South Florida was founded on a simple but powerful belief: healing happens in relationship. When a person in recovery cares for a rescued animal, something remarkable takes place. Responsibility becomes routine. Routine becomes stability. And stability becomes a new life.

Our model pairs sober living residents with animals from shelters and rescue organizations. Residents participate in daily animal care as part of their recovery program, gaining structure, emotional connection, and a renewed sense of purpose.

Connected to Positive Recovery, our 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization, we are building a network of recovery residences across South Florida, starting with our first home in Broward County.

Services & Programs

Built Around What Works

Our programs integrate evidence-based recovery practices with animal-assisted support, creating a holistic path toward long-term sobriety and personal growth.

01

Structured Sober Living

A safe, substance-free residential environment with house guidelines, curfews, and accountability measures designed to support your recovery journey.

  • Drug and alcohol-free residence
  • House meetings and peer support
  • Random testing and accountability
  • Life skills development
02

Animal-Assisted Recovery

Our signature program pairs each resident with a rescued animal. Daily caregiving builds routine, empathy, and emotional regulation through genuine connection.

  • One-on-one animal pairing
  • Daily feeding and care routines
  • Veterinary education workshops
  • Adoption pathway for animals
03

Recovery Support Services

Beyond housing, we connect residents with the resources they need to rebuild every part of their lives, from employment to emotional wellbeing.

  • Recovery meeting coordination
  • Employment readiness support
  • Peer mentorship program
  • Community reintegration planning
Why Jimmy Safe Place

Recovery That Goes Beyond the Ordinary

We are not just another halfway house. Our approach is built on the science of connection, the discipline of structure, and the healing power of caring for another living being.

FARR Accreditation Pathway

We are actively pursuing accreditation through the Florida Association of Recovery Residences, ensuring our facility meets the highest standards for safety, ethics, and quality of care in sober living.

The Pet Therapy Difference

Research consistently shows that animal interaction reduces anxiety, lowers cortisol levels, and increases oxytocin. Our residents experience these benefits daily through hands-on animal care and companionship.

Real Community, Real Support

Our residents are not just housemates. They share responsibility, support each other through challenges, and build the kind of genuine relationships that sustain long-term recovery.

A True Safe Haven

Jimmy Safe Place is exactly what the name says. A place where people and animals both find safety, structure, and the chance to start over. No judgment. Just support and a path forward.
